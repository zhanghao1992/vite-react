时间表达

What time is it?

It's 7 o'clock.

It's 8:30.

核心句型

It's time to + 动词

get up / go to school

It's time for + 名词

English class / lunch

日常词汇

get up / breakfast

go to school / lunch

maths / art / PE

区分 "time to" 和 "time for"
